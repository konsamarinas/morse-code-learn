Symmetrical symbols and the last letters.


One more lesson left to complete the english alphabet. In this lesson we will learn 6 more letters. Let’s start with symmetrical symbols. We have 3 symbols that if we place an axis of symmetry in the middle, we will notice that they are symmetrical to each other. These symbols are: K, P and R.



And here we have the last 3 symbols left that do not follow any rule. These symbols are: C, J and Z.
