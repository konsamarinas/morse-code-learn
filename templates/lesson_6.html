Punctuation marks


   We have come to the last lesson which is punctuation marks in Morse code. This lesson is a bit difficult because there is no pattern to remember. But we will try to make you remember them with some methods. Let's look at the first 6 punctuation marks...

   . .-.-.-
   ; -.-.-.
   , --..--
   ? ..--..
   ' .----.
   - -....-

   As you will notice we have divided these 6 punctuation marks into 3 pairs (red, blue, green) and each color group has two symbols of which the codes are upside down. For the remaining 10 punctuation marks, unfortunately, there is no memorization method. You should try to learn them as they are. Here is the table...


   ! -.-.--
   ( -.--.
   ) -.--.-
   & .-...
   : ---...
   = -...-
   + .-.-.
   _ ..--.-
   $ ...-..-
   @ .--.-.
