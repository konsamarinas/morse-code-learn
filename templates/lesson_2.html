The upside down symbols.


There are some symbols that if we read them upside down another letter is formed. For example (.-) and (-.). Dividing them into this category helps us to memorize them more easily because if we do not remember a letter and we remember its couple we can find out it. So let's look at the pairs we have:


A N
B V
D U
F L
G W
Q Y

So if we know that (A) is (.-) we can find what symbol is (N) that obviously is (-.). Let’s look the following table:


PAIR LETTER 1 SYMBOL 1 LETTER 2 SYMBOL 2
1 A . - N - .
2 B - . . . V . . . -
3 D - . . U . . -
4 F . . - . L . - . .
5 G - - . W . - -
6 Q - - . - Y - . - -
